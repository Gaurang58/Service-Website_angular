Payment Gateway

A payment gateway is a technological solution that enables fast and securepayment processing of card-not-present (CNP) transactions. It is a link between the seller, the buyer, and the bank, similar to a POS terminal at an offline store. Thanks to the gateway, it’s all done quickly and securely. So, in a way, it’s a digital “twin” of a physical point-of-sale, which is a payment terminal at a physical shop’s counter.
